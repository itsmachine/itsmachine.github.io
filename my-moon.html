My moon

And the moon

I see everyday

In all of her ways

Large cheeks

Shiny blue eyes

As my spider

She's in her web

She has a web is my entire heart

She puts me to sleep

Her shine 

From far 

Whispers in my ears

From my window

Wherever she is

I just hope she hears me

From my fall

As I stare 

Between I sleep

Into her eyes.
