Godly masterpiece

No person

Will ever have the power

To break 

Or separate

The two souls

That were created

Harmonized

Joined

Combinated

Amalgated 

United

By the hands of Gods

Against all odds

Risks

Evils

Circumstances,

Planned 

Through a lifetime

For a lifetime.

And if these soul break out,

It wasn't the choice of a person

The bond will always be there

It is only through the death of a person

Through evil, or the devil,

Killing their own heart

Would anyone ever be able

To 

Ignore the beauty

The holyness

The divinity

The mission

In themselves,

In each other,

In their love.

Playing out

We are immortal pieces of gold

That nothing can divide

As true Love

The fountain of Life

The greatest masterpiece

God has ever created,
