God's path

I like to think of myself as a true believer,

And the reason for so is that I simply believe,

I believe in God, and that is enough.

Whenever I try

In the calculations of a scientific machine

Find the odds of an impossibility

For disbelief,

God himself descends from the sky

It shows himself in the

Small yet genuine

Divine miracles of Life.

And for simply believing,

Life is not so frightneing,

Neither does it look like

A pointless play of none.

Instead, God shows Life

As it really is

Without 

Whatever God puts as test

When it hides the choice 

For a true life

He doesn't play with dices

Neither are we his experiment mice,

He gives a chance

To see truth

Build trust

Live correctly

As the terrain waves of water

That slowly turns us into ascending

Into God's own path.
