The map

As the days go

I'm not afraid

I'm not ashamed

Of walking

At the usual

The heavy block of metal

Ignoring the cattle

Executing

A mind focused

Somewhere

In plans

Strategies 

Imaginations

Eyes.

Because

In fact

Hidden are my dreams

My smile

My colors

My joy

The world can't see it

Because I keep it with her

She has my treasure

And whenever we meet

We bath in yellow, red and laugh

And I take with me the map

The map I see everyday, everytime

To our beings

To our spirits

To our dreams

To our hearts

To our warmth

To our love.
