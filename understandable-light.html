Understandable light

The sun is cheerful
The sun is bright
The sun is magnificent
Under any wind
Under any water
Under any weather

You can try to,
In vain,
Classify each kind of sunlight according to the spectrum of light
Statistically determine how damaging the sunlight is
Understand the vicious social constructs behind the concept of sun
Blame the sun for all the economic inequality of the world,
or
Just
Experience it,
Enjoy it.

When things are made out of light,
There is nothing to deconstruct
There is nothing to interpret,
Everything is understood
Everything is simplified,
Every thing is.
