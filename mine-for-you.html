Mine for you

Dear dear sunshine

When you wonder

Across the skies

Lost between

Our crossed eyes,

Where happens

Where did it come?

All this beauty....

All this whole...

Burning from my heart

And all for you,

It's simple to say...

Simple to understand...

All mine for you

Only you and me,

In the other side of the sea

It hides in our way

A wooden small treehouse

As we are birds

We must rest our wings

In our warm sweet home

And we shall thrive

And we shall see

And we shall build

Beyond everything

For you are mine

And I am yours

All mine for you

You're my sweat home.
