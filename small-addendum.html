Small addendum

I started this place as an attempt to write poems and grasp my feelings, but it's gradually expanded and, from this moment forward, different format and content will be included.

"Technology"/"innovation"/"social tech" took a huge toll in my life. As of right now, I need a place to record this process, and I'll be adding more autobiographical elements in the future.

Hence, also more criticism, Philosophy, Economics, Theology.

I have no intentions in doing so.

Perhaps, this will save me in a court of law. Perhaps, this will help autistic teenagers in 2070. Perhaps, it'll be ridiculed by masses of apathetic ironic cows. Perhaps, these words will die with me, lost in Google's crawler.

Regardless, the truth yells, it must get out, desperately, uncertain of the future. 

Likes are not alike.
