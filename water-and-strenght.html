Water and strength

Strenght
Alone
Surely isn't
Exactly a force.
Strength,
Like water,
Feeds
Nurture the soul
Yet
All its molecules
All that energy
It doesn't know where to
It disperses everywhere
It may go the wrong place
It may create a flood.
That's why
Beside the water
We all need a hose
Or a splinkler
Or a cup
Beside the strength
There is the need of belief
A road
A stair
Upwards to the skies
Funneling all water
All energy
All electricity
To the right space
A calm, nurturing
Growing, lasting
Enduring heaven
Where strength
Finds the right way
And builds the way
