Resonance

Do you blame me?
I can't stop
Keep following my own tail
The toes dance with myself
Cardiac fibers twitch the smile
Facial fibers twitch the heart.

Either is that
I have a big
Big lonely heart
Or she has a strong
Strong honey voice.

It lives in me the echo
The reverberation, the scintilla,
It reflects, moves, waits
And then
The resonance.

The gem words
Tsunamis the soul,
Explode the eyes.
The heart expands,
Embraces the body.

Each step merely becomes
Extension of the true spirit,
Endless aromas added
In the bottomless trunk
Of vibrant colorful sights.
