Dangerous words

 

The best and most dangerous words and characters

Are those of innocence

Truthfulness

Hope

Genuinity

As tiny particles of God himself

These are not simply written

Or painted in the back of a head,

These particles

By God himself

They are carved

In the heart of a soul

From life to eternity

And there is no enough amount

Of brainwashing

Of lobomotomy

Of calculation

Of argumentation

That can wash away their essence

With its own slow time

Mistakes by these can be made

And in penitence they surely will pay

But their Truth will always stand out

Intact, without any doubts

 

And when the day comes

Near or apart

They will understand

And in such see the Light that exists

There was never evil

There were just mistakes

Regret or smile

It's a question of patience

It's a question of understanding.
