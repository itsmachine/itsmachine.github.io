Coherent limbs

I've been thinking for a long time why this repository exists in the first place.

If I am a ghost and these are my thoughts, what does it matter if they're here or in the echos of my mind?

And I'm not here to help anyone.

And I'm not here to amuse anyone.

If you find me on 4chan or Reddit, it doesn't matter.

If you harassen, ridiculize me, it doesn't matter.

If you like me, want to pay me, want to meet me, it doesn't matter.

It's not why I'm here.

I'm here to be forced, obligated, to compile, summarize, shorten words.

It's my only organ where coherence exists.

May it appeal to your tongue, be served.
