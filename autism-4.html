Autism 4

<

Since I was born
Everytime of my day
I'm in a movie
It's a cheesy but candid mixture of
Romance, heroism and truthfullness
I smile at the story
I wave
I run
I write poems and paint to it
And I pretty much do everything
For today and for tomorrow
Even a bit too much
But
Every once in a while
I just get the blank stare.

It's a weird feeling
I'm part of a movie
I'm inside a movie
And then I'm not
And then I can't be heard
And then I'm just in the audience
And then I'm just a blind child playing scenes in my head
And all my energy
All my actions
They are ignored
They are patronized
They are all analyzed
Judged
Manipulated
Bullied
Not as fullhearted and authentic actions
Trying to part of a formidable story
The brightest and most colorful of them all
Unlike one there will ever be seen.

Instead
My actions
Are seen as
Overlooked
Isolated
As those of a
Retarded
Reckless
Weak
Spectator
And the more
I move
I run
I yell
Louder and louder
The more I suffocate in my own words
The less Life I can breathe
The more tears are found in me
The less life has the world.
