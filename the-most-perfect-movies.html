The most perfect movies

Perfect movies
Are
Just
Perfect
No issues, only fabricated smiles
That's not
Good enough,
As plotted lines
Words
Chapters
Characters
All tender
Smooth
Static
Unreal
Complacently
The by-product of time
Alienated
An artificial creation
Of wonderless minds.

The magnificent movies
Really are
The real ones
Shouts, screams, conflicts
Regrets, reconciliations, last-minute amazement
The simple reality of existence
Desperately
Eagerly
From the heart
Seeking any freckles of perfection
Swirling through the halls of beauty
Guided through the winds of good
The last minutes
The last seconds
Overwhelming.

It makes the real
The genuine
The authentic
The most surprising
The most beautiful
As
The most imperfect
Of all perfections
Is
The most perfect
Of them all.
