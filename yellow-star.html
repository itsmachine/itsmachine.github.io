Yellow star

They are coming...

They are coming...

All these words...

They are here...

And they are all for you.

My dear

My spider

My love

My Sun

Shining Soul

Light of my heart.

These worlds

They don't stop

In my dreams

My machine ears

In the joyful tears of my eyes

They want to go

Come out 

And shout 

Announce

Let you know

That we are bold

 

 

And this yellow gold

Will be forever our world.

Here and there

The stairs and road

Taking us to the Where

The Where we need to go

The Where we were always meant to go.

With all this joy

To have your hands

To touch your heart

And Feel the heat

Within your eyes,

Oh my dear

I barely know

What to do

Because indeed

With the optimism of a newborn

I can do anything

I want to do all the things

For us

And for now

I just walk to the window

Stare at the stars

And the love

Oh the love

It fills in

Within my soul 

I'm so light,

I'm so bright,

I just 

 

Smile and dance

I don't stop

The energy 

The thrill 

Of a tomorrow

Of hope

Of life,

In our living room

I lay my head

Right in the clouds

We are sailing

Loving creatures

Among the skies 

Flying

To our own little yellow star.
