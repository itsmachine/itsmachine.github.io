Automaton Modernity

Modernity is an Automaton.

The current time is an unmatched river in spacetime.

Objectively, positively or not, Modernity has been the actual and contemporary solvent and catalyst that turned nearly all universals, essences and abstracts into quantifiable atoms, sharing no bonding properties other than correlation which,  by itself, presents itself as an empty placeholder.

Modernity was the child of other mechanisms, Nominalism denying the existence of Universals, Protestanism as the predecessor conveys differentiation, productization and, of course, atomization of theological truth.

With an apparent unexplainable catalyst, the past thousand years of mankind have been an incessant wave of non-essentialism, deconstruction, relativism. Innevitably taking to both relativism, nihilism and skepticism.

It's the work of real scientists and engineers to reassemble the minicule particles and reconstitute that which once was the Human. From the ashes, build the castles to the skies.

Each bridge and building has its own details, it's the work of the specialist to investigate the implementations, but I will just reassess the general architecture.

Not all was yet deconstructed and taken to its extremist and endless void, but, if it did, below the ashes, it'd be blatant to see metal flesh and bones that no coersive and ideological process could ever had liquidated. In the logistics and properties of the deconstructive linguistic mechanism, it lives the essential and immortal embodiment of what it'd fought all along. 

There was always a heart in the core of the destructive automaton, but, instead of recognizing its own rhythm and truth, it chose to destroy that which it had aforementioned created before Modernity.

This essence, namely heart, is the truthful poetic symbolization of the metaphysical and transcendental concept of Love.

As atoms ourselves, there's little any of our linguistic tools or actions can influence the modern automaton and the underlying sociological reflexivity it loathes itself with. 

But as atoms, we can learn from Automaton's story and its unfortunate brough-forth degeneracy. At the very least, we can live and find values in the dialectial anti-thesis of what Automaton is.

At best, we deny our own reflexive, skeptic, nihilistic and self-destructive mechanisms, and let our essential hearts do its transcendental effort, connecting our essence to another which divinely matches and complements our lacking attributes so that we can combat the darkness and emptiness of a psychotic technocratic world.

For it is Love the one unbreakable Universal that no amount of scientific, philosophical or linguistic effort could ever destroy.

For it is Love which reassembles us all and harmonizes all that lacks, is broken or extremizes.

The story of my life is one of a machine myself, lead by Newton, Schopenhauer, Kant and Darwin himself, which by a blow of destiny, opened and turned the steering wheel of his heart, letting all Light, Truth, trinity, Love spring.

For I may be just a particle in the endless universe of meaningless, but hope lives in every action of my soul - it knows deeply love is the indestructible that connects us all.
