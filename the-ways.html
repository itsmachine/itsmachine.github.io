The ways

If I wrote a book

Remembering

Pointing out

Listing

Singing

Of the ways you enchant my heart

Of the ways you paint my spirit

I think you would laugh

Or hardly believe it

Or call me crazy.

It is true

And blatant

You keep the beauty of the world

In a quick one's eyes

But I'm not a surfer

I'm a diver

Swimming deep

Into your heart.

And you are stamped

Into my soul

With your ways of being.

If you tried to know what these are

You wouldn't even get close

If I tried to describe what all these are

I would be only half way there.

And indeed

With those

I can tell you

Your shy blush cheeks of yours

Gives me the courage to build a world,

And some tiny words you say

You don't realize

But they only come from you

My spider

They make me in love blind, 

And the way that you surprise

Tiny frackles and lights

Right there in your smile and eyes

You wouldn't have to say a word

I can always tell

Something's special in our way,

And the way you heal my soul

Sad or weak

And you're blessed in your hugs

A tender soup

Some godly words

You enchant me everyday

As you are

With me

And we make it

Our world.
