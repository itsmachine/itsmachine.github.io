My only watch

In this big and crazy life of mine, I never cared much to be anywhere, at a anytime.

That's why I've only had a single watch.

In fact,

My only watch has no numbers, pointers or bullets.

My only watch doesn't track seconds, hours, or days.

My only watch is not an object.

My only watch takes me to understand what time is.

My only watch awakens me to the misteries of time.

My only watch freezes and, then melts time.

My only watch gives meaning to the time it doesn't count.

My only watch makes time itself be timeless.

My only watch perfects the beats of my heart.

My only watch gets me sleep, then awake, every morning sun.
