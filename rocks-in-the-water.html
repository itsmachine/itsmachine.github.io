Rocks in the water

In the river
Through the currents
Time goes
Then appears
Out of nowhere
Out of the sky
Suddenly a rock
Changes the flow
The currents they adjust
The fishes
Afraid
They say
Why God?
Why this now?
What do I do?

The time it goes
The rock it sits
The water it flows
A different trail
A new rhythm
A better flux
The fishes now
Swimming in glee
Thrilled
They say
Thank God
All this now?
How did you do?
