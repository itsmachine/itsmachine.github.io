Your hand by my side

Oh spider

From the blue eyes

Of my yellow dreams

I write you words

i want to sing you a song

Here just now

And just everyday

Let our nights

Shape our clay,

Our hearts together

Have their own speak

Harmonize in gold

And wouldn't need

No other word.

I may murmur

Or dance

Or serenade

Or play the piano

Or play the guitar

Indeed try everything

Living without love

Is not really to live

And as such

You have my wishes

My sacrifices

My sweat

My dreams

And I don't expect

Anything other than

Just your hand

By my side.
